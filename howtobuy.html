How to Buy

1Go to the app store and download the app, Trust Wallet.

2Purchase ARB or USDT (Arbitrum) via the app, or deposit to your address. Note that you need to have ETH (Arbitrum One Network) to pay for fees.

3Go to the DApps tab at the bottom and find SushiSwap. iPhone users will have to enable the browser within TrustWallet by typing trust://browser_enable within Safari.

4On Sushi Swap, in the "From" slot select ARB or USDT, depending on which one you bought. In the bottom slot tap "Select currency" to search, and then paste in the contact address on this page

5Click the icon with the dials at the top left, then set the slippage tolerance to 11-12%, and close that interface once done.

6Put the amount of USDT you wish to spend in the "From" box, and press the "Swap" button.
