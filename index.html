Baby Pepe Coin

Baby Pepe ($BPEPE) is a deflationary token designed to become scarcer over time. Being a refection reward token, Baby Pepe ($BPEPE) holders will earn reflection reward in Arbitrum that will be automatically sent to their wallet.

Baby Pepe birthed by fans of the $Pepe meme community. It’s simply for fun, to share love, pet, and increase reward from pepe earnings and other meme coins.

Baby Pepe (PEPE) is community-centric thus everyone who interacts with this platform will earn a passive profit for buying, holding or promoting $BPEPE.

Features

Presale

Baby Pepe ($BPEPE) will be available for sale using a presale mechanism under the most secured Presale Launchpad; Pinksale.

Liquidity

As a community-centric meme coin over 50% LP will be provided on Pancakeswap from Presale. Baby Pepe (PEPE) promotes cryptocurrency decentralization and as such trade/trading is not controlled or regulated by any third party.

Community Oriented

The driving force of Baby Pepe ($BPEPE) is our community growth and development. 20% token allocation will be distributed strictly for massive marketing, partnership, collaborations and promotion while rewarding our community with incentives.

Low Slippage

Outrageous tax has proved to be a dwindling factor for token adoption, ownership and community acceptance. A low slippage will benefit our community as well as improve buy or sell. Baby Pepe ($BPEPE) has a low slippage and this will promote token growth.
